Fuel
------

Wooden Slabs can be used as fuel for the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace, smelting 1.5 items per slab block.

Notes
-------

* Rotten Plank Slabs that generate on the Sludgeon Tower may be rigged as falling blocks, meaning they have a chance to fall if a player walks on or underneath them. A falling block can be indicated by the particles falling from it. The block will become unprotected after it falls.
