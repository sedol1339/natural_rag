Dentrothysts ingame

A hollowed Giant Weedwood Tree, showing the concentration of Dentrothyst inside

**Dentrothysts** are unique amber-like blocks that can be found uncommonly in scattered 1-3 block veins within the trunks of Giant Weedwood Trees, including Stumps. There are two varieties - a more common Green Dentrothyst, and a rarer Orange Dentrothyst.

Collection
------------

When harvested normally, Dentrothysts drop 4 Dentrothyst Shards of their corresponding type. The blocks themselves must be harvested with a Silk Touch tool to be collected directly.

Trivia
--------

* The word "Dentrothyst" is a portmanteau of the words "dendrite" (crystal with a treelike structure) and "amethyst" (a gem).
