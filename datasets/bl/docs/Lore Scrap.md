# Lore Scrap

[Aside block] Lore Scrap
------------------------

### Type

Other

### Renewable

No

### Stackable

No

### Compostable

No

### ID

lore\_scrap

**Lore Scraps** are unique, readable items that can be found as Loot from pots and chests in Caverns, Lake Caverns, Ruins, Underground Ruins, Idol Head Statues, Shrines, Tar Pool Dungeons, Cragrock Towers, and the Wight Fortress.

Reading
---------

Lore Scraps can be viewed with a right-click and closed with a right or left-click. There are 10 different title and page variants for Lore Scraps that can be found, which either tell stories or information about the mod and the lore surrounding it, and often times show a picture as well.
