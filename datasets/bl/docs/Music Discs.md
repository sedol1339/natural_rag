# Music Discs

### Type

Other

### Renewable

Varies

### Stackable

No

### Compostable

No

### ID

See Disc Types

There are 15 **Music Discs** added to the Betweenlands mod. They are all treasure from animating Item Scrolls, and they can also be found as Loot from chests in Shrines, Cragrock Towers, and the Wight Fortress. The Frosty Disc can additionally be found as Loot from Presents during the Winter seasonal event. The only exception is Deep Water, which is obtained through other methods.

### Deep Water Disc
The Deep Water disc cannot be obtained through Item Scrolls or chests like the other **Music Discs**. The method for obtaining it is hidden below in a spoiler tab; *DO NOT* open this tab if you do not wish to be spoiled.

| Obtaining Deep Water Disc (Spoiler Warning) |
| --- |
| The Deep Water disc is obtained by placing an Urchin in a Weedwood Jukebox. Deep Water will play, and when the jukebox is emptied it will drop the disc. |

Acquiring Music
-----------------

All Music Disc tracks are available as .ogg files in the Betweenlands jar file, under assets/thebetweenlands/sounds/records. Additionally, you can visit Voog2's ReverbNation song list or the Angry Pixel Soundcloud page for all tracks (including unused ones).
