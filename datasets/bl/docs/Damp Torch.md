Any non-Betweenlands Torch that is placed in The Betweenlands fizzles out and become a **Damp Torch**, which provides no light. This encourages the use of Sulfur Torches in the dimension.

Placement
-----------

Damp Torches can only be placed on the top and side of most blocks. Breaking the block a Damp Torch is sitting on will cause the torch to break as well.
