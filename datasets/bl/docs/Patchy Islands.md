# Patchy Islands

Map Color

**Patchy Islands** are Betweenlands biomes characterized by lush, low-lying islands surrounded by Swamp Water, with flat shores of Silt, several Weedwood, Hearthgrove, and Sap Trees, and Giant Bulb-Capped Mushrooms dotted about.

Composition
-------------

Patchy Islands biomes consist of:

* Swamp Grass
* Swamp Dirt, directly under the surface
* Mud, on the sea floor
* Middle Gem Ores, rarely on the sea floor
* Betweenstone Pebble, on land or underwater
* Blue Eyed Grass
* Boneset
* Cardinal Flower
* Cattail
* Tall Cattail
* Milkweed
* Moss, in scattered patches
* Shoots
* Swamp Reed, on land next to or sticking out from the water
* Swamp Tallgrass
* Algae, in patches on the water
* Bog Bean, sticking out from the water
* Golden Club, sticking out from the water
* Marsh Marigold, sticking out from the water
* Mire Coral, rarely underwater
* Swamp Water, as seas between islands
* Menhir, rarely

Mobs
------

The following mobs can spawn naturally on the surface in Patchy Islands biomes:

* Dragonfly
* Firefly, when the Rift event is active at night or inactive
* Mire Snail
* Frog
* Gecko
* Sporeling, only on Shelf Fungus
* Anadia (green), in water
* Greebling
* Greebling Rider, in the sky
* Lurker, in water
* Angler, in water
* Swamp Hag
* Wight
* Silt Crab, only on Silt
* Blood Snail
* Leech
* Chiromaw
* Shambler
* Emberling, only on Hearthgrove Logs, rarely

Structures
------------

The following structures can generate in Patchy Islands biomes:

* Weedwood Tree, on land or partially submerged in the water
* Hearthgrove Tree, on land or partially submerged in the water
* Sap Tree, occasionally
* Giant Bulb-Capped Mushroom, occasionally
* Silt Beach, lining island edges
* Idol Head Statue, partially submerged in the water, rarely
* Underwater Ruins, partially or entirely submerged in the water, rarely
