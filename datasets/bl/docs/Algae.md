Algae ingame

**Algae** is a plant-like block that generates in patches on the surface of Swamp Water in Swamplands, Patchy Islands, Coarse Islands, and Deep Waters biomes. It slows the player down when swam through.

Collection
------------

Algae must be harvested with Syrmorite Shears to be collected. When harvested with a Sickle, it instead drops an Algae Clump, which can then be ground into Ground Algae.

Compostability
----------------

Algae has a compost process time of 4 minutes and 10 seconds and a compost amount of 3. Visit the Compost page for more information regarding the composting mechanic.
