**Giant Hollow Logs** are natural structures found in Swamplands biomes. They are giant, felled hollow logs with holes, often covered with the various plants found in the biome.

Mobs
------

While no mobs in particular spawn in or around Giant Hollow Logs, breaking the Rotten Bark the log is composed of has a chance to release a large Termite.
