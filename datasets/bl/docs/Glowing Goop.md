### Jellyfish
Glowing Goop drops from Jellyfish and Cave Jellyfish.

Placement
-----------

Glowing Goop can be placed on any face of a solid block. Hold right click and release to throw it onto far away blocks. The projectile will emit a shader light while falling. It can also be placed **underwater**.

Providing Light
-----------------

Glowing Goop provides a light level of 12, slightly less than a Sulfur Torch. Unlike Sulfur Torches, it will not go out when exposed to Heavy Rain.
