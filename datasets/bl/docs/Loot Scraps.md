### Animating Recipe
Attempting to animate a Fabricated Scroll has a chance to "fail", simply returning some of the Loot Scraps used to make it.

| Result | Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Loot Scraps** (1-3) (2/5 weight) | Fabricated Scroll + Sulfur (16) + Life Crystal fuel (16 charge) |  |

### Trimming
Loot Scraps can be obtained from trimming any *treasure* Anadia item (that is not rotten) in the Trimming Table. The amount obtained per item depends on the Anadia's Size attribute, with larger sizes yielding more scraps.
