# Advancements

The Betweenlands mod adds a huge variety of new **Advancements** to the game, categorized under 7 different tree tabs. Each tab pertains to a specific area of the mod's features, and some advancements are hidden until completed.

The following is a list of Betweenlands advancement trees and the Advancements they contain.

Contents
--------

* 1 Adventurer
* 2 Survivalist
* 3 Craftsman
* 4 Miner
* 5 Farmer
* 6 Fighter
* 7 Herbalist
* 8 Trivia
* 9 History

Trivia
--------

* The advancement Onions Have Layers, Tools Alike is a direct reference to the 2001 dreamworks film, Shrek. At one point the main character, Shrek, tells his sidekick Donkey that ogres are like onions, saying: "Ogres are like onions, onions have layers, ogres have layers".
