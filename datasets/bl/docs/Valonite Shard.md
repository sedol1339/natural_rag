### Animating Recipes
Valonite Shards have a chance to be found as treasure from animating Item Scrolls or Fabricated Scrolls.

| Result | Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Valonite Shard** (1-3) (12/193 weight) | Item Scroll + Sulfur (16) + Life Crystal fuel (16 charge) |  |
| **Valonite Shard** (1-3) (12/193 weight \* 3/5) | Fabricated Scroll + Sulfur (16) + Life Crystal fuel (16 charge) |  |

### Drops
Valonite Shards drop from breaking Valonite Ore with an Octine Pickaxe or better.

### Loot
Valonite Shards can be found as Loot from:

* Pots of Chance in Cragrock Towers and the Wight Fortress.
* Urns of Chance in the Sludgeon Tower, Labyrinthine Vaults, Barrishee Lair, and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcoves in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Weedwood Chests in Idol Head Statues, Shrines, Cragrock Towers, the Wight Fortress, and the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.

They can also be found as scattered items in Chiromaw Matriarch Nests.
