Usage
-------

A Filtered Silt Glass Jar can store up to 4 buckets of fluid. Right-click the jar with a fluid container to store the fluid. When broken, the fluid is kept, and the amount is displayed in the item's tooltip. Fluid can be retrieved by right clicking with an empty fluid container such as a bucket or Weedwood Bowl if the fluid is compatible.
