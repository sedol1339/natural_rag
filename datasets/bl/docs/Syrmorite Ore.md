Syrmorite Ore in-game

**Syrmorite Ore** is an ore that, once smelted, can be used to make mid-tier equipment and for other uses.

Obtaining
-----------

### Generation
Syrmorite Ore generates uncommonly underground between Y-layers 105 and 85 within Betweenstone, mostly in the upper parts of the Cavern layer.

### Drops
Syrmorite Ore can drop rather uncommonly from Boulder Sprites.

Trivia
--------

* The name of the ore is a reference to a YouTuber known as Syrmor, who was the first youtuber to do a Let's Play of the mod (Early Alpha version).
* Syrmorite was originally called Blurite, as the developers quickly needed a name, and the ore was blue. Coincidentally, it also happened to be the name of an ore in the game Runescape.
