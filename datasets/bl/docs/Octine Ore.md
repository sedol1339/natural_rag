Octine Ore in-game

**Octine Ore** is an ore that, once smelted, can be used to make mid-tier equipment and create fire, and for other uses.

Obtaining
-----------

### Generation
Octine Ore generates somewhat uncommonly underground between Y-layers 70 and 45 within Betweenstone, in the lower parts of the Cavern layer.

### Drops
Octine Ore can drop somewhat rarely from Boulder Sprites.

Properties
------------

### Particles
Small fire particles will constantly emanate from Octine Ore.
