# Underwater Ruins

Description
-------------

Underwater ruins are small, decrepit structures that generate submerged in deep water biomes near cragrock spires. They are mostly made up of variants of cragrock and pots of chance.

Composition:
--------------

Cracked Cragrock tiles

Cragrock tiles

Chiselled Cragrock

Mossy Cragrock tiles

Cragrock bricks

Pot of Chance

Loot:
-------

For loot, the underwater ruins use underwater\_ruins\_pot
