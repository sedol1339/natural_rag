# Music

The Betweenlands mod contains a plethora of custom **Music** tracks, of which the implemented ones all currently play while in the Betweenlands dimension. They are a combined list of songs composed by Voog2, Scarecrowman, and Rotch Gwylt.

The Betweenlands
------------------

The Betweenlands OST

### Menu Tracks
The following tracks play on the custom menu screen for the Betweenlands mod:

* A Foreboding Welcome (Rotch Gwylt)
* The Adventure Begins (Rotch Gwylt)
* This Is Where It Starts (Rotch Gwylt)

### Ambient Tracks
The following tracks play randomly while in The Betweenlands dimension:

* Barrow Mounds (Scarecrowman)
* Crocodile Tears (Voog2)
* Darkness (Voog2)
* Don't Follow the Whisps (Scarecrowman)
* Emerald Embers (Scarecrowman)
* Enter the Mire (Scarecrowman)
* Ghostfaces (Scarecrowman)
* In Between (Voog2)
* Incantation (Scarecrowman)
* Leech Love - Part 1 (Voog2)
* Leech Love - Part 2 (Voog2)
* Murk Beneath a Twinkling Void (Scarecrowman)
* Numbskull (Scarecrowman)
* Rip in the Fold (Voog2)
* Spore Ballad (Voog2)
* The Other World (Voog2)
* The Quietus (Scarecrowman)

### Boss Tracks
The following tracks play while fighting bosses or mini-bosses, namely the Dreadful Peat Mummy, Primordial Malevolence, Barrishee, and Pit of Decay:

* Barrishee (Rotch Gwylt)
* Dreadful Peat Mummy (Rotch Gwylt)
* Pit of Decay (Rotch Gwylt)
* Primordial Malevolence (Rotch Gwylt)

### Music Disc Tracks
Visit the Music Discs page for a list of tracks used as music discs.

Eternal Melodies
------------------

Eternal Melodies OST

While the following tracks are not included in the mod by default, they are included as part of the Eternal Melodies music addon mod by **Rotch Gwylt** and TheCyberBrick:

### Menu Tracks
* Back Home (Rotch Gwylt)

### Ambient Tracks
* A Walk Through the Marsh (Rotch Gwylt)
* Eternal (Rotch Gwylt)
* Light Will Follow (Rotch Gwylt)
* Rowing Through The Fog (Rotch Gwylt)
* Strange but Pleasant (Rotch Gwylt)
* We Are the Ones Who Crawl (Rotch Gwylt)

### Structure Tracks
The following tracks play while in specific structures:

* Labyrinthine Vaults (Rotch Gwylt)
* Wight Fortress (Rotch Gwylt)

### Boss Tracks
* Chiromaw Matriarch (Rotch Gwylt)
* Emberling Shamans (Rotch Gwylt)
* Spirit Tree (Rotch Gwylt)

Note that the Eternal Melodies addon only supports Release 3.9.5 of The Betweenlands and later.

Acquiring Music
-----------------

The full album of used tracks can be acquired from the Betweenlands Original Game Soundtrack Spotify page or in the widget below. Profits made from Spotify royalties will be donated to the development team's charity of choice.

These are some other available sources:

* All used default tracks are available as .ogg files in the Betweenlands jar file, under assets/thebetweenlands/sounds.
* Visit the Angry Pixel Soundcloud page for all tracks by Voog2 (including unused tracks).
* Visit the Scarecrowman Soundcloud page or the Between Dimensions YouTube Playlist for all tracks by Scarecrowman.
* Visit Rotch Gwylt's YouTube Playlist for all default tracks by Rotch Gwylt (including old tracks).
  + Visit his Eternal Melodies YouTube Playlist or his Bandcamp Page for all addon tracks.
