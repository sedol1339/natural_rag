**Bottled Shallowbreath** is an item used to fuel the Draeton. It can be right-clicked into the Draeton's burner to refill it's fuel by 1/4th.

It can also be stored in barrels.

Collection
------------

Bottled Shallowbreath can be obtained by killing a Shallowbreath with a Dentrothyst Vial by punching it with left-click. The vial only needs to do the finishing blow, so players can first weaken the Shallowbreath with another weapon.
