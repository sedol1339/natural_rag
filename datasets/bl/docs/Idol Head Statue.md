**Idol Head Statues** are structures that generate uncommonly in Patchy Islands biomes. They are giant, ruined statue heads shaped like idols, and come in three visual variants. Each contains a single chest with loot.

Idol Head Statues have a world location that identifies the area they take up.

Composition
-------------

Idol Head Statues consist of:

* Cragrock
* Smooth Cragrock
* Smooth Cragrock Slab
* Smooth Cragrock Stairs
* Cragrock Brick Slab
* Cragrock Brick Stairs
* Moss, on the top and sides
* Lichen, on the top and sides
* Block of Octine, as the eyes of the statue
* Weedwood Chest, buried near the bottom of the statue

Loot
------

The Chest inside an Idol Head Statue uses idol\_heads\_chest as its loot table.
