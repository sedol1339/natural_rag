# Biomes

The following is a list of all **Biomes** that are added by the Betweenlands mod.

Biomes
------

* Coarse Islands: Sparse collections of steeply cut islands, Cragrock, and massive Root formations.
  + Raised Isles: A variant of Coarse Islands containing islands high in the sky, held up by tall Roots.
* Deep Waters: Large water bodies filled with underwater life, broken occasionally by Cragrock Spires.
* Marsh 0: Broken flats of Mud, Peat and water, inhabited by fog, poisonous clouds, and hidden terrors.
  + Marsh 1: A variant of Marsh containing smaller landmasses and even more fragmented terrain.
* Patchy Islands: Sparse collections of relatively flat islands brimming with bright and colorful flora.
* Sludge Plains: A vast plain of Mud and Sludgy Dirt, complete with Tar pools and Rotten Weedwood Trees.
  + Sludge Plains Clearing: A sub-biome of Sludge Plains containing the Sludgeon.
* Swamplands: Relatively thick groups of Weedwood Trees, with many plants and decent landmass.
  + Swamplands Clearing: A sub-biome of Swamplands containing the Spirit Tree.
