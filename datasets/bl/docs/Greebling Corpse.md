# Greebling Corpse

**Greebling Corpses** are the decaying bodies of deceased Greebling Riders, found in Chiromaw Matriarch Nests. They are surrounded by insect particles.

Looting
---------

Greebling Corpses can be looted by left clicking them, though they will not always yield loot when this is done. Each corpse will drop items twice, but these drops are not necessarily distinct. Once a corpse has dropped loot twice, it will disappear.
