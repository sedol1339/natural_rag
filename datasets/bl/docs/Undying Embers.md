**Undying Embers** are items primarily used in making draeton burners, and taming emberlings.

Obtaining
-----------

### Drops
Undying Embers can drop from normal Emberlings upon death if killed by a player, and drop from Emberling Shamans upon death.

Uses
------

### Taming
Undying Embers can be right-clicked on an Emberling to feed it, immediately causing it to become tamed.

### Fuel
Undying Embers can be used as fuel for any Furnace (including the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace), smelting 100 items per piece.
