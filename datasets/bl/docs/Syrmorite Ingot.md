### Animating Recipes
Syrmorite Ingots have a chance to be found as treasure from animating Item Scrolls or Fabricated Scrolls.

| Result | Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Syrmorite Ingot** (5-12) (20/193 weight) | Item Scroll + Sulfur (16) + Life Crystal fuel (16 charge) |  |
| **Syrmorite Ingot** (5-12) (20/193 weight \* 3/5) | Fabricated Scroll + Sulfur (16) + Life Crystal fuel (16 charge) |  |

### Trimming
Syrmorite Ingots can be obtained from trimming a *treasure* Anadia item with Slender or Stubby body type (that is not rotten) in the Trimming Table. In the case of the Stubby body type, the amount obtained per item depends on the Anadia's Size attribute, with larger sizes yielding more ingots.

### Loot
Syrmorite Ingots can be found as Loot from Weedwood Chests in Idol Head Statues, Cragrock Towers, the Wight Fortress, and the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.
