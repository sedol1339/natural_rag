### Trimming
Syrmorite Nuggets can be obtained from trimming a *treasure* Anadia item with Skittish body type (that is not rotten) in the Trimming Table. The amount obtained per item depends on the Anadia's Size attribute, with larger sizes yielding more nuggets.

### Water Filtering
Syrmorite Nuggets can be obtained from filtering Stagnant Water with a Mesh Strainer in the Water Filter.

### Drops
Syrmorite Nuggets can drop occasionally from Crypt Crawlers upon death.

### Loot
Syrmorite Nuggets can be found as Loot from:

* Pots of Chance in the Cavern and Lake Cavern layers, Marsh Ruins, Underwater Ruins, Underground Ruins, Tar Pool Dungeons, Cragrock Towers, and the Wight Fortress.
* Urns of Chance in Sludge Plains Ruins and the Sludgeon Tower, Labyrinthine Vaults, Barrishee Lair, and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcoves in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Weedwood Chests in Idol Head Statues, Shrines, Cragrock Towers, the Wight Fortress, and the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.

They can also be found as scattered items in Chiromaw Matriarch Nests.
