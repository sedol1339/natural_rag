### Generation
Compacted Mud generates in large quantities on the surface of the Sludge Plains Clearing sub-biome. It can also be found in the Labyrinthine Vaults, Winding Walkways, Crypt Tunnels, Crypt, and Pit of Decay of the Sludgeon.

Properties
------------

### Particles
Small mud droplet particles will occasionally drip down from Compacted Mud when there is no block directly below it.

### Plant Support
Compacted Mud supports the placement and growth of basic plants.

### Movement
Unlike regular Mud, Compacted Mud does not cause players or mobs to sink into it.
