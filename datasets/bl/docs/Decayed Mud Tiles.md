Decayed Mud Tiles ingame

**Decayed Mud Tiles** are special blocks that are found in the Labyrinthine Vaults, Crypt, and Pit of Decay of the Sludgeon. They have a normal variant and a Cracked variant, both of which use connected textures.

Contents
--------

* 1 Plant Support

Plant Support
---------------

Both variants of Decayed Mud Tiles support the placement and growth of basic plants.

Movement
----------

Both variants of Decayed Mud Tiles are slippery, causing players, mobs and items to slide across them.
