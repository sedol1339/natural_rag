Plants, Fungi, and Corals

  • Algae: A flat plant-like block that grows just above water.
  • Arrow Arum: A red, arrow-tipped plant that spawns in Marshes.
  • Barnacle Cluster: An aquatic organism that can be grown and harvested.
  • Black Hat Mushroom: Small mushrooms with black caps that spawn in Swamplands.
  • Bladderwort: A pink-flowered plant that grows upwards from large water bodies.
  • Blue Eyed Grass: Low-lying grass with blue buds that spawns in Patchy Islands.
  • Blue Iris: A blue flower that spawns in Coarse Islands.
  • Bog Bean: A white-flowered plant that spawns above water in Patchy Islands.
  • Boneset: A white flower that spawns in Patchy Islands.
  • Bottlebrush Grass: A sparse grass that spawns in Marshes.
  • Broomsedge: Large, broom-shaped grass that spawns in Marshes.
  • Bulb-Capped Mushroom: Glowing blue mushrooms that spawn around their giant variant.
  • Bulb-Capped Mushroom Cap: The cap block of Giant Bulb-Capped Mushrooms.
  • Bulb-Capped Mushroom Stalk: The stalk block of Giant Bulb-Capped Mushrooms.
  • Button Bush: Small little bushes that spawn in Marshes.
  • Cardinal Flower: Large, bright red flowers that spawn in Patchy Islands.
  • Cattail: Small reeds that spawns in most surface locations of the Betweenlands.
  • Cave Grass: Low-lying grass that is found in the deep Lake Caverns of the Betweenlands.
  • Cave Moss: Hanging, vine-like moss that grows in the Caverns of the Betweenlands.
  • Copper Iris: A copper orange flower that spawns in Coarse Islands.
  • Cryptweed: A strange hanging plant that grows in the Sludgeon.
  • Dead Grass: A brownish grass block that is found commonly in Swamplands.
  • Dead Weedwood Bush: A withered bush that spawns in Sludge Plains.
  • Deep Water Coral: A glowing blue coral found in Deep Waters.
  • Double Swamp Tallgrass: A tall, curly grass found in Patchy Islands.
  • Edge Leaf: Small leaves that are found on the edges of walls in the Sludgeon.
  • Edge Moss: A small mossy growth that is found on the edges of walls in the Sludgeon.
  • Edge Shrooms: Brown fungus that is found on the edges of walls in the Sludgeon.
  • Fallen Leaves: Flat leaf blocks that spawn below Weedwood Trees in Swamplands.
  • Flathead Mushroom: Small mushrooms with broad tan caps that spawn in Swamplands.
  • Giant Root: The wood of the Giant Roots found in Coarse Islands.
  • Golden Club: A long, yellow-flowered plant that spawns above water in Patchy Islands.
  • Hanger: Long vine-like plants that grow from Giant Weedwood Trees.
  • Hearthgrove Leaves: The leaf block of the Hearthgrove Tree.
  • Hearthgrove Log: The log of the Hearthgrove Tree found in Patchy Islands.
  • Hearthgrove Sapling: The sapling of the Hearthgrove Tree, which can be grown.
  • Hollow Log: Fallen, rotten logs that spawn in Marshes and Sludge Plains.
  • Lichen: A white, bacterial organism that grows on Cragrock Spires and other locations.
  • Marsh Hibiscus: A white and red flower that spawns in Marshes.
  • Marsh Mallow: A bush with white flowers that spawns in Marshes.
  • Marsh Marigold: A bush with yellow flowers that spawns above water in Patchy Islands.
  • Milkweed: A magenta flower that spawns in Patchy Islands.
  • Mire Coral: A glowing orange coral found in large water bodies.
  • Moss: A cover plant that grows in many locations in the Betweenlands.
  • Nesting Block: Scraggly, bushy blocks that make up the Nests of Chiromaw Matriarchs.
  • Nettle: A dark green bush that stings, spawns in most surface locations of the Betweenlands.
  • Nibbletwig Leaves: The leaf block of the Nibbletwig Tree.
  • Nibbletwig Log: The log of the Nibbletwig Tree found in Swamplands.
  • Nibbletwig Sapling: The sapling of the Nibbletwig Tree, which can be grown.
  • Pale Grass: Low-lying, transparent grass that is found in the Sludgeon.
  • Peat: A block of decomposed plant matter that spawns in Marshes.
  • Phragmites: Tall, reed-like plants that spawn in Marshes.
  • Pickerel Weed: A bluish-violet flower that spawns in Marshes.
  • Pitcher Plant: Tube-like carnivorous plants that spawn in Swamplands.
  • Poison Ivy: A poisonous, vine-like plant that grows from small Weedwood Trees.
  • Portal Frame: The gray wood that spawns as part of the Portal Tree.
  • Root: Twisty roots that break the surface in Coarse Islands and Sludge Plains.
  • Root Pod: Pods dropped by Root Sprites, which can be grown into Roots.
  • Rotbulb: A disgusting fungal growth that is found in the Sludgeon.
  • Rotten Bark: Rotting bark that makes up large hollow logs and dead trees.
  • Rubber Tree Leaves: The leaf block of the Rubber Tree.
  • Rubber Tree Log: The log of the Rubber Tree, which can be tapped for Rubber.
  • Rubber Tree Sapling: The sapling of the Rubber Tree, which can be grown.
  • Sap Tree Leaves: The leaf block of the Sap Tree.
  • Sap Tree Log: The log of the Sap Tree which drops edible sap.
  • Sap Tree Sapling: The sapling of the Sap Tree, which can be grown.
  • Shelf Fungus: A fungus block that grows on the sides of Giant Weedwood Trees.
  • Shoots: Small bright green plants that spawn in Patchy Islands.
  • Sludgecreep: A low-lying brown plant that spawns in Sludge Plains.
  • Soft Rush: A basic grass plant that spawns in Marshes.
  • Spirit Tree Leaves: The leaves of the Spirit Tree, which sometimes bear fruit.
  • Spirit Tree Log: The log of the ominous Spirit Tree.
  • Spirit Tree Sapling: The sapling of the Spirit Tree, which can be grown into a friendly sentry tree.
  • String Roots: Stringy, hanging roots that grow in the Sludgeon.
  • Sundew: A red-dotted carnivorous plant that spawns in Marshes.
  • Swamp Grass: The primary grass block of the Betweenlands.
  • Swamp Kelp: A plant that grows upwards in large water bodies.
  • Swamp Plant: A basic leafy plant that spawns in Swamplands.
  • Swamp Reed: A plant that grows upwards in many locations in the Betweenlands.
  • Swamp Tallgrass: A low-lying grass that spawns in most surface locations of the Betweenlands.
  • Tall Cattail: A taller variant of the Cattail that spawns in Patchy Islands.
  • Tall Sludgecreep: A slightly taller variant of Sludgecreep that spawns in the Sludgeon.
  • Thorns: A stingy plant that grows like vines in the Caverns of the Betweenlands.
  • Venus Fly Trap: A classic carnivorous plant that spawns in Swamplands.
  • Volarpad: A massive plant with a large pad that spawns in Swamplands and Coarse Islands.
  • Water Weeds: A low-lying water plant that spawns in Deep Waters.
  • Weedwood: The inner part of Weedwood Trees that can be used in crafting.
  • Weedwood Bush: Leafy bushes found in Swamplands and Coarse Islands.
  • Weedwood Leaves: The leaf block of the Weedwood Tree.
  • Weedwood Log: The outer blocks of the Weedwood Tree.
  • Weedwood Sapling: The sapling of the Weedwood Tree, which can be grown.
  • Weeping Blue: A tall blue flower with decay-restoring properties, spawns in Marshes.

Crops

  • Aspectrus Plant: An artificial plant that allows one to grow and farm Aspects.
  • White Pear Plant: A plant that bears edible white fruit when grown.
  • Yellow Dotted Fungus: A farmable and edible fungus.

Minerals

  • Betweenlands Bedrock: The Bedrock of the Betweenlands, found at the world's bottom.
  • Betweenstone: The primary stone of the Betweenlands, used in many recipes.
  • Coarse Swamp Dirt: A version of Swamp Dirt that grass cannot spread to.
  • Compacted Mud: A version of Mud that does not cause sinking.
  • Cragrock: A bluish stone that is found in Coarse Islands and as part of spires in Deep Waters.
  • Dentrothysts: An amber-like crystal found within Giant Weedwood Trees.
  • Life Crystal Stalactite: Pitstone spikes containing white ore found in the Lake Caverns.
  • Limestone: A white stone that spawns in large patches in the Betweenlands Caverns.
  • Middle Gem Ores: A variety of colored gems found within Mud in the Betweenlands.
  • Mud: A thick, entity-sinking block found everywhere in the Betweenlands.
  • Octine Ore: An uncommon, flaming orange ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
  • Pitstone: A dark stone that spawns deep in the Lake Caverns of the Betweenlands.
  • Scabyst Ore: A somewhat rare pale blue ore found deep underground in the Betweenlands.
  • Silt: A slowing, sand-like block found on beaches in Patchy Islands.
  • Slimy Bone Ore: A relatively common fossil ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
  • Sludgy Dirt: A slowing brown block found in patches in Sludge Plains.
  • Solid Tar: A dense, solidified block of tar created when Swamp Water and Tar meet.
  • Stalactite: Twisty stone spikes that spawn in the Caverns of the Betweenlands.
  • Sulfur Ore: A common yellow ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
  • Syrmorite Ore: An uncommon blue ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
  • Swamp Dirt: The primary dirt block of the Betweenlands.
  • Valonite Ore: A rare pinkish ore found deep underground in the Betweenlands.

Liquids

  • Puddle: Thin blocks of Swamp Water left behind by Heavy Rain.
  • Rubber: Liquid rubber obtained by tapping a Rubber Tree.
  • Stagnant Water: A light greenish-brown glowing water found in Caverns in the Betweenlands.
  • Swamp Water: The basic water block found everywhere in the Betweenlands.
  • Tar: A thick, black liquid that spawns in specific pools in the Betweenlands.

  • Chiseled Betweenstone: Decorative carved blocks for Betweenstone.
  • Chiseled Cragrock: Decorative carved blocks for Cragrock.

  • Deep Water Coral Block: A decorative block of Deep Water Coral.

  • Middle Gem Blocks: Compacted blocks of one of the three Middle Gems.

  • Mud Bricks: Decorative brown brick blocks.
  • Mud Brick Shingles: Decorative brown roof tile blocks.
  • Mud Tiles: Decorative mud-based tile blocks.
  • Nibbletwig Planks: Planks crafted with Nibbletwig Logs.
  • Pitstone Bricks: Decorative brick blocks for Pitstone.
  • Pitstone Pillar: Decorative pillar blocks for Pitstone.
  • Pitstone Tiles: Decorative tile blocks for Pitstone.
  • Polished Dentrothysts: Glass-like, smoothed versions of Dentrothyst.
  • Polished Dentrothyst Panes: The pane variants of Polished Dentrothyst.
  • Polished Limestone: A smooth decorative block for Limestone.
  • Purified Swamp Dirt: An artificial dirt block useful in farming.
  • Reed Mat: A decorate carpet that can be used to sit.
  • Rotten Planks: Planks crafted with Rotten Bark.
  • Rubber Block: A compact block of solid Rubber that has a bouncy effect.
  • Rubber Tree Planks: Planks crafted with Rubber Tree Logs.
  • Scabyst Bricks: Decorative brick blocks made out of Scabyst.
  • Scabyst Lined Pitstone: Decorative Pitstone blocks etched with Scabyst.

  • Slimy Bone Block: A compacted block of Slimy Bones.
  • Slopes: Decorative stair-like blocks useful in roofing.
  • Smooth Betweenstone: Smooth decorative blocks for Betweenstone.
  • Smooth Cragrock: A smooth decorative block for Cragrock.
  • Smooth Pitstone: A smooth decorative block for Pitstone.
  • Stairs: Decorative stairs for the Betweenlands.
  • Thatch: A decorative block made with Dried Swamp Reed.
  • Walls: Decorative walls for the Betweenlands.
  • Weedwood Chip Path: Flat travel blocks that speed entities walking on them.
  • Weedwood Planks: Planks crafted with Weedwood or Weedwood Logs.
  • Weedwood Walkway: Travel blocks that speed entities walking on them.
  • Worm Pillar: Decorative mud-based pillar blocks.

Utility

  • Alembic: A block capable of distilling Infusion Buckets into completed Infusions and Aspect Vials.
  • Animator: A block capable of using the power of Life Crystals to 'animate' certain items.
  • Aspect Vials: Vials containing a specific aspect usable in infusing.
  • Barrels: Blocks capable of storing and carrying any liquid.
  • Buckets: Can be placed to tap the logs of Rubber Trees for liquid rubber.
  • Buttons: Functioning buttons for the Betweenlands.
  • Censer: A block capable of producing ground-laying fog from various sources, with varying effects.
  • Climbing Rope: A block that can be climbed on and 'roped down'.
  • Compost Bin: A block capable of turning plant matter into Compost to use in farming.
  • Crab Pot: A block capable of catching Silt and Bubbler Crabs with a lure.
  • Crab Pot Filter:A block allowing caged Crabs to filter certain items.
  • Dark Druid Altar: The altar in the middle of the Druid Circle that assembles the Swamp Talisman.
  • Dentrothyst Vials: Containers for Infusions.
  • Doors: Functioning doors for the Betweenlands.
  • Dual Sulfur Furnace: A variant of the Sulfur Furnace that can smelt twice as many items at once.
  • Dug Purified Swamp Dirt: Created by digging a hole in Purified Swamp Dirt with a shovel.
  • Dug Purified Swamp Grass: Created when grass spreads to Dug Purified Swamp Dirt.
  • Dug Swamp Dirt: Created by digging a hole in Swamp Dirt with a shovel.
  • Dug Swamp Grass: Created by digging a hole in Swamp Grass with a shovel.
  • Fence Gates: Functioning fence gates for the Betweenlands.
  • Fish Trimming Table: A block capable of trimming certain caught mobs.
  • Fishing Tackle Box: A small portative chest the player can place and sit on.
  • Gecko Cage: A block capable of housing a gecko which can be tested on to discover Aspects.
  • Glowing Goop: A light source that can be thrown.
  • Infuser: A block capable of mixing various ingredients together to make raw Infusion Buckets.
  • Item Shelf: A storage block that can display and store multiple items.
  • Lanterns: Light sources for the Betweenlands that, unlike torches, are not extinguished by rain.
  • Menhir: A naturally generating monolith that can be linked to act as a waypoint.
  • Mortar: A block capable of grinding many materials into their ground forms.
  • Moss Bed: A bed that sets a player's spawn point in the Betweenlands.
  • Mud Brick Flower Pot: A Betweenlands variant of the Flower Pot that can also be used as a candle.
  • Pressure Plates: Functioning pressure plates for the Betweenlands.
  • Purifier: A block capable of 'purifying' certain blocks into new forms.
  • Repeller: A block that can keep mobs away by putting up a repelling shield.
  • Silt Glass Jar: A block used to store Fish Bait.
  • Simulacra: Naturally generating statuettes that contain mysterious effects.
  • Smoking Rack: A block capable of smoking various types of food.
  • Smoldering Peat: Created by lighting Peat on fire.
  • Sulfur Furnace: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Furnace.
  • Sulfur Torch: Torches that function properly in the Betweenlands, but are extinguished by rain.
  • Syrmorite Hopper: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Hopper.
  • Trapdoors: Functioning trapdoors for the Betweenlands.
  • Weedwood Chest: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Chest.
  • Weedwood Crafting Table: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Crafting Table.
  • Weedwood Jukebox: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Jukebox.
  • Weedwood Ladder: A functioning ladder for the Betweenlands.
  • Weedwood Lever: A functioning lever for the Betweenlands.
  • Weedwood Sign: A writable Sign for the Betweenlands.
  • Wind Chime: A block capable of predicting most Events in the Betweenlands.

Generated

  • Angry Betweenstone: Betweenstone etched with the symbol of the mod's creators.
  • Bauble: Experience-filled ornaments that randomly appear and disappear on trees during Winter.
  • Beam Lens: Transmits beams from the origin and can be reoriented to direct the beam a certain way.
  • Beam Origin: A block that generates a beam for the puzzle on top of the Sludgeon Tower.
  • Beam Passage: Allows beams from the origin to pass through from above, but cannot be reoriented.
  • Black Ice: Frozen, slippery swamp water that spreads during Winter.
  • Brazier: Can be lit on fire to activate the Beam Origin at the top of the Sludgeon Tower.
  • Broken Mud Tiles: A block that crumbles into Stagnant Water when walked on, found in the Sludgeon.
  • Carved Rotten Bark: Rotten Bark etched with decorative runes, found in the Sludgeon.
  • Decayed Mud Tiles: Blocks that are slippery to walk on, found in the Sludgeon.
  • Dungeon Alcove: Alcoves in the walls of the Sludgeon that can contain urns with loot.
  • Dungeon Door: Massive doors that can be opened using a rune combination.
  • Dungeon Door Code: Contains the rune combination that must be used to open Dungeon Doors.
  • Energy Barrier: Blocks that guard certain areas of the Wight Fortress and the Sludgeon.
  • Glowing Betweenstone Tile: A decorative light source found in the Wight Fortress.
  • Glowing Smooth Cragrock: A decorative light source found in Cragrock Towers.
  • Item Cage: An energy-imbued cage used in a special ritual, found in the Wight Fortress.
  • Mirages: Blocks that look ordinary but can be passed through, used to conceal specific secrets.
  • Monster Spawner: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Monster Spawner.
  • Offering Table: Stands of offering containing loot, which have a special connection with Simulacra.
  • Possessed Block: A block housing a terrifying jumpscare, found in the Wight Fortress.
  • Pot of Chance: Pots containing various loot, found in Caverns and structures in the Betweenlands.
  • Present: Gift-filled presents that randomly appear and disappear during Winter.
  • Puffshroom: A block housing a fungal surprise, found in the Sludgeon.
  • Rotten Support Beam: Decorative support beams found in the Sludgeon.
  • Rune Stone: Overworld Stone etched with various symbols, found in the Druid Circle.
  • Runged Mud Bricks: Brown brick blocks that can be climbed on, found in the Sludgeon.
  • Sludge: A slowing cover block left behind by Sludges as they hop about.
  • Snow: A cover block that builds up during Winter snowfall.
  • Spike Traps: Blocks that house deadly spikes, found in the Wight Fortress and the Sludgeon.
  • Urn of Chance: Urns containing various loot, found in the Sludgeon and other mud-based structures.
  • Wall Candle: Light sources that can be lit or unlit, found in the Sludgeon.
  • Weak Blocks: Blocks that break when walked upon, found mainly in the Wight Fortress.
  • Wisp: Colored flames found eerily in the Betweenlands.

Unimplemented

  • Bloody Temple Brick: A decorative bloodstained variant of Temple Bricks.
  • Bronze Circle Brick: A decorative block notifying an idol type found in Ancient Temples.
  • Chiseled Temple Brick: A decorative carved variant of Temple Bricks.
  • Corrupt Betweenstone: A variant of Betweenstone found in the Prime Wight's Domain.
  • Cracked Temple Brick: A decorative cracked variant of Temple Bricks.
  • Dungeon Tile: A decorative block found in Ancient Temples.
  • Dungeon Wall: A decorative block found in Ancient Temples.
  • Gold Circle Brick: A decorative block notifying an idol type found in Ancient Temples.
  • Mossy Temple Brick: A decorative overgrown variant of Temple Bricks.
  • Silver Circle Brick: A decorative block notifying an idol type found in Ancient Temples.
  • Slimy Dirt: The primary dirt block of the Snail Realm.
  • Slimy Grass: The primary grass block of the Snail Realm.
  • Smooth Temple Brick: A decorative smooth variant of Temple Bricks.
  • Temple Bricks: A unique, decorative brick block found in Ancient Temples.
  • Temple Pillar: A decorative pillar variant of Temple Bricks.
  • Totem Block: A block that sustains fire, found in Tribe Settlements.
