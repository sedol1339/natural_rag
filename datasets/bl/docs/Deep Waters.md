Composition
-------------

Deep Waters biomes consist of:

* Swamp Water, as large, deep seas
* Mud, on the sea floor
* Middle Gem Ores, rarely on the sea floor
* Betweenstone Pebble, underwater
* Lichen, on land, primarily on Cragrock Spires
* Algae, around Cragrock Spires on the water
* Bladderwort, sticking out from the water
* Swamp Kelp, underwater
* Water Weeds, underwater
* Deep Water Coral, underwater
* Mire Coral, underwater
* Barnacle Cluster, underwater, on Cragrock Spires
* Wisp, occasionally
* Menhir, rarely on land, including on Cragrock Spires

Mobs
------

The following mobs can spawn naturally on the surface in Deep Waters biomes:

* Firefly, when the Rift event is active at night or inactive
* Sporeling, only on Shelf Fungus
* Anadia (silver), in water
* Greebling Coracle, on water
* Greebling Rider, in the sky
* Freshwater Urchin, in water
* Jellyfish, in water
* Lurker, in water
* Angler, in water
* Rock Snot, in water

The following mob can spawn in the Lake Cavern layer, but only when it is below a Deep Waters biome:

* Cave Jellyfish, in water

Structures
------------

The following structures can generate in Deep Waters biomes:

* Cragrock Spire, partially submerged in the water, alongside Algae, Lichen, and Barnacle Clusters
* Underwater Ruins, partially or entirely submerged in the water, rarely
