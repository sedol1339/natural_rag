# Cave Jellyfish

[Aside block] Cave Jellyfish
----------------------------

### Health

5 
### Damage

Easy/Normal: 2 
Hard: 4 
### Drops

Glowing Goop

### Experience

0

### ID

jellyfish\_cave

**Cave Jellyfish** are aggressive mobs that spawn in the Lake Cavern layer.

Behavior
----------

Cave Jellyfish will swarm around the player and deal electrical damage. The more Cave Jellyfish are nearby, the faster they can attack.
