Swamp Kelp in-game

**Swamp Kelp** is a tall plant that grows underwater.

It has an item form that is primarily used in making fried swamp kelp. It is considered a Plant Item, and can be ground into Ground Swamp Kelp.

Obtaining
-----------

### Generation
Swamp Kelp generates underwater in Coarse Islands, Raised Isles, and Deep Waters biomes.

### Loot
Swamp Kelp items can be found as Loot from Pots of Chance in Underwater Ruins.

Properties
------------

### Particles
Small particles resembling water bugs will occasionally swim around Swamp Kelp.

### Growing
Swamp Kelp will naturally grow upwards, and will grow infinitely up to the surface of the water, where it will stop growing.

### Breaking
Breaking a lower part of a Swamp Kelp growth will break all parts above it as well.

Drops
-------

When broken, Swamp Kelp drops a **Swamp Kelp** item. The blocks themselves cannot be collected.

Item Properties
-----------------

### Planting
Swamp Kelp items can be used to plant Swamp Kelp on plant-supporting blocks that are in Swamp Water, as well as directly above itself under the same conditions.

Uses
------

### Compostability
Swamp Kelp items can be composted in a Compost Bin. They have a compost process time of 4 minutes and 10 seconds and a compost amount of 3. Visit the Compost page for more information regarding the composting mechanic.
