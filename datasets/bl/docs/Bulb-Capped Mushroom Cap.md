Particles
-----------

Small particles resembling flies, moths and mosquitoes will occasionally float around Bulb-Capped Mushroom Caps.

Collection
------------

When harvested normally, Bulb-Capped Mushroom Caps usually drop nothing, but have a chance to drop a Bulb-Capped Mushrooms item, which can then be ground into Ground Bulb-Capped Mushroom.

Bulb-Capped Mushroom Caps themselves must be harvested with a Silk Touch tool to be collected.

Fuel
------

Bulb-Capped Mushroom Caps can be used as fuel for the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace, smelting 1.5 items per cap block.
