# The Prime Wight's Domain

***Note: The following content is NOT currently implemented in the mod. Information is highly susceptible to being false, incomplete, and/or otherwise inapplicable.***

**The Prime Wight's Domain** is a dimension that is currently unimplemented. It is planned to be the lair of the Prime Wight, the final boss of the Betweenlands mod. Corrupt Betweenstone will likely be found here, but most other details are unknown.
