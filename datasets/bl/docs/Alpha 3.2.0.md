# Alpha 3.2.0

*This is a snapshot of the upcoming 1.12.2 update. This is not the final version! Changes are to be expected and some things may not be fully implemented yet.*

**Alpha 3.2.0** was a snapshot update to the **Betweenlands** for Minecraft 1.12.2 that was released on December 14, 2017. It added Seeded Hangers, as well as a huge number of changes and bug fixes.

Changelog
---------

### Changes

* Added missing translations to Herblore Book
* Increased Lore Scrap and Aspectrus Seeds drop rate
* Reduced Pot of Chance hardness, now breaks faster with a pickaxe
* Added Frosty Disc to Present loot table
* Sporelings now have a small chance to spawn riding a Gecko
* Snowfall during Winter is now a seperate event (id: thebetweenlands:snowfall)
* Added missing Aspects to Bladderwort items
* Reduced Poison Ivy and Nettles gen rate
* Increased Mushrooms, Volarpad and Weedwood Bush gen rate
* Added underground Hangers gen
* Increased Underground Ruins gen rate
* Decreased Syrmorite Ore and Octine Ore gen rate
* Octine Ore now only generates further down
* Players can now slowly walk through Weedwood Bushes
* Shockwave Sword no longer breaks completely, but becomes useless when at 0 durability
* All Betweenlands tools and armour can now be repaired in the Animator
* Added config for Rotten Food whitelist
* Slightly reduced flight speed of Ring of Ascent
* Reduced fertilizer effectiveness on Aspectrus Plant
* Wights now have a chance to buff a nearby Swamp Hag
* Location titles now have a one minute cooldown
* Two rings can now be equipped at once
* Added more JEI compat
* Polished Dentrothyst Pane recipes now give 16 items
* Increased Pitcher Plant and Venus Fly Trap gen rate
* Biome mob spawner cooldowns are now saved
* Added Cake to Rotten Food blacklist
* Added Herblore Book merging recipe
* Added Seeded Hangers that drop White Pear Seeds and grow on Giant Weedwood Trees

### Bug Fixes

* Fixed blocks haven't incorrect BlockFaceShape's, causing snow and torches to be placable on all sides
* Fixed Moss Bed dropping vanilla Bed
* Fixed Betweenlands Snow having incorrect hardness
* Fixed Rubber Tap still using up Reed Rope
* Fixed Animator taking more than one item when fed by a Hopper
* Fixed Giant Hollow Logs generating incorrectly
* Fixed Sporelings not rotating when falling down
* Fixed Primordial Malevolence blockade not causing damage and suffocating
* Fixed sound not playing when Infusions finish
* Fixed Swamp Hags not knowing how to walk
* Fixed broken Scabyst coating recipe
* Fixed indirect damage sources not working properly with Middle Gems
* Fixed incorrect Weedwood Bush pick item
* Fixed Aspectrus Plant having a way too low growth rate
* Fixed Aspectrus Plant missing collision box
* Fixed some projectiles not blockable by shields
* Fixed BL Fences trying to connect to BL Walls
* Fixed Dark Druid Altar sound only playing once and not stopping properly
* Fixed Aspect Vial amount rounding issue
* Fixed brick and chiseled variant recipes giving too many items
* Fixed crops being harvestable with Sickle or Syrmorite Shears
