# Giant Bulb-Capped Mushroom

*Not to be confused with Bulb-Capped Mushroom or Bulb-Capped Mushrooms.*

### Generation

Patchy Islands  
Underground Oasis

**Giant Bulb-Capped Mushrooms** are natural structures that generate in Patchy Islands biomes, as well as rarely within Underground Oases. They are the most reliable source of the glowing mushroom variety.

Composition
-------------

Giant Bulb-Capped Mushrooms consist of:

* Bulb-Capped Mushroom, clustered around the giant mushroom
* Bulb-Capped Mushroom Stalk, as the stalk block
* Bulb-Capped Mushroom Cap, as the cap block

Growing
---------

Giant Bulb-Capped Mushrooms cannot be grown.
