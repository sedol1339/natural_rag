# Staff of the Mist Walker

The **Staff of the Mist Walker** is a unique item that can be found as treasure from animating Item Scrolls.

Animating Recipe
------------------

| Result | Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Staff of the Mist Walker | Item Scroll + Sulfur (16) + Life Crystal (13% charge) |  |

Usage
-------

Right clicking with a Staff of the Mist Walker creates a bridge of mist blocks that can be walked across in front of the player for 12 seconds. There is a 12 second cooldown between uses.

Repairing
-----------

Unlike most other equipment with durability, there is no way to repair a damaged Staff of the Mist Walker.
