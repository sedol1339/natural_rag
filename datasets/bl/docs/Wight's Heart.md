# Wight's Heart

[Aside block] Wight's Heart

**Wight's Hearts** are consumable items that restore health. They are also used in making animators, tar beast hearts, and a couple other features, as well as recharging life crystals.

Obtaining
-----------

### Drops
Wight's Hearts can drop from Wights upon death if killed by a player.

### Loot
Wight's Hearts can be found as Loot from:

* Pots of Chance in the Cavern and Lake Cavern layers, Marsh Ruins, Underwater Ruins, Underground Ruins, Cragrock Towers, and the Wight Fortress.
* Urns of Chance in Sludge Plains Ruins and the Sludgeon Tower, Labyrinthine Vaults, Barrishee Lair, and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcoves in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Weedwood Chests in Idol Head Statues, Shrines, Cragrock Towers, the Wight Fortress, and the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.

Properties
------------

### Consuming
When consumed, Wight's Hearts restore 8 health.

#### Recharging
Wight's Hearts can be used to recharge depleted, whole Life Crystals. Each Wight's Heart applied to the crystal restores 16 charge points to it, and any number between 1 and 8 can be crafted with the crystal at once (as long as the resulting charge is not higher than 128).

| Result | Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Charges Life Crystal (16 charge per heart) | Shapeless: **Wight's Heart** (1-8) + Life Crystal (less than full charge) |  |
