# Chiromaw Matriarch Nest

[Aside block] Chiromaw Matriarch Nest
-------------------------------------

### Type

?

### Generation

Raised Isles

The **Chiromaw Matriarch Nest** is a large roost that can be found infrequently atop Raised Islands in the Raised Isles. It contains loot, Chiromaw Hatchlings, and Greebling Corpses, and is protected by a Chiromaw Matriarch.

The nest has a world location that identifies the area it takes up, as well as a title that appears briefly to players who approach it.

Most of the blocks of the Chirowmaw Matriarch Nest are protected from destruction by a ward that persists until the player stands upon the Cragrock block at the nest's center.

The inside of an average Nest.

Composition
-------------

Chiromaw Matriarch Nest's consist of:

* Cragrock
* Nesting Block
* Dead Moss
* Edge Shrooms
* Edge Leaf
* Rotbulb
