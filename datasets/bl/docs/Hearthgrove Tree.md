# Hearthgrove Tree

[Aside block] Hearthgrove Tree
------------------------------

### Type

?

### Generation

Patchy Islands

A partially submerged Hearthgrove Tree

**Hearthgrove Trees** are a type of tree that generates commonly in Patchy Islands biomes. They can be anywhere between 11 and 15 blocks tall and between 7 and 15 blocks wide. They feature a wide leaf canopy drooping with hangers and a thin, branching trunk with a few outspreading bottom roots.

Hearthgrove Trees are particularly useful for their fuel-efficient, tarrable logs.

Composition
-------------

Hearthgrove Trees consist of:

* Hearthgrove Log, as the log block
* Hearthgrove Leaves, as the leaf block
* Hanger, hanging from the leaves (unseeded)

Mobs
------

Emberlings can rarely spawn on the Hearthgrove Logs found as part of the roots of Hearthgrove Trees in Patchy Islands biomes.

Growing
---------

Hearthgrove Trees can be grown with Hearthgrove Saplings.
