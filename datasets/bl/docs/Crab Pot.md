Usage
-------

Must be placed underwater. Right click with a Silt Crab or Bubbler Crab item to place the crab in the pot. There needs to be water above Crab Pot or else crabs won't enter it.

### In-game Tooltip
Add Anadia Remains to tempt Crabs. Can be placed on top of a Crab Pot Filter for more uses.

### Baiting
Any item can be placed in a Crab Pot. Any Anadia Parts can be used to lure crabs. Right click or destroy the pot to remove the crab as an item.
