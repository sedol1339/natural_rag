Betweenstone Tiles ingame

**Betweenstone Tiles** are craftable building blocks. They have a normal variant, a Mossy variant, and a Cracked variant. In addition to being crafted, the normal variant can also be found as part of Marsh Ruins, Shrines, and Tar Pool Dungeons, and all variants can be found as part of the Wight Fortress.
