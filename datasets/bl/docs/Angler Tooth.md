**Angler Teeth** are items primarily used in making arrows and fishing spears.

They can be ground into Ground Angler Tooth.

Obtaining
-----------

### Water Filtering
Angler Teeth can be obtained from filtering Swamp Water with a Mesh Strainer in the Water Filter.

### Drops
Angler Teeth can drop from Anglers upon death if killed by a player.

They can also drop from looting Greebling Coracles.

### Loot
Angler Teeth can be found as Loot from Pots of Chance in Underwater Ruins.
