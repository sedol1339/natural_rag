### Drops
Balls of Sap drop from breaking any Sap Tree Log.

They can also drop from Leeches upon death.

### Loot
Balls of Sap can be found as Loot from:

* Pots of Chance in the Cavern and Lake Cavern layers, Marsh Ruins, Underground Ruins, Cragrock Towers, and the Wight Fortress.
* Urns of Chance in Sludge Plains Ruins and the Sludgeon Tower, Labyrinthine Vaults, Barrishee Lair, and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcoves in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Weedwood Chests in Idol Head Statues, Shrines, Cragrock Towers, the Wight Fortress, and the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.
* Offering Tables that sometimes generate next to Simulacra throughout The Betweenlands.

Properties
------------

### Consuming
When consumed, Balls of Sap restore 2 decay and 0.4 decay saturation.

They are not affected by Food Sickness.

Uses
------

### Censing
Balls of Sap can be used as an effect source for the Censer. When fueled, this will slowly restore the decay bar of any player in a 65x65x19 area around the Censer, at a rate of 1 decay () restored every 5 seconds. The effect is indicated by a small brown puff. Each Ball of Sap can restore decay up to 2 times for a total of 2 decay (), and will not be used up until this decay restoration is absorbed.
