**Pheromone Thoraxes** are items primarily used in infesting weedwood bushes to produce silk grubs and silk grub cocoons.

Normal Pheromone Thoraxes can be crafted into a **Clutch of Pheromone Thoraxes**, which is primarily used in making pheromone extract and as storage.

### Drops
Pheromone Thoraxes can drop from Infestations upon death.

Uses
------

### Infesting
Normal Pheromone Thoraxes can be right-clicked on a Weedwood Bush to infest it, turning it into a Pheromone Infused Weedwood Bush.

Clutches of Pheromone Thoraxes can be right-clicked on a Weedwood Bush to over-infest it, turning it into a Dead Weedwood Bush and causing it to instantly spawn an Infestation.
