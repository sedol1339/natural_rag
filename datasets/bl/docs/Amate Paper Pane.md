Amate Paper Panes ingame

**Amate Paper Panes** are craftable, slightly translucent blocks that are slightly thicker than other panes. They have a **Connected** variant that uses connected textures, a **Squared** variant, and a **Rounded** variant.

Torch Support
---------------

Unlike other types of panes, Amate Paper Panes support the placement of torches, including Sulfur Torches, on top of them.
