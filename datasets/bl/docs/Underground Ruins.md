*"I've been down in the caverns again, quite deep now where there's lakes of water and scattered ruins. Some of them had decent loot, but I was more worried about the things that were looking for me rather than the things I was looking for."*

> *-From Explorer's Entries: Underground Ruins*

**Underground Ruins** are a variety of small structures that generate in Lake Caverns. They are partially intact constructs that have six different variations.

Underground Ruins have a world location that identifies the area they take up.

Composition
-------------

Underground Ruins consist of:

* Pitstone Bricks
* Pitstone Brick Slab
* Pitstone Brick Stairs
* Pitstone Brick Wall
* Pitstone Tiles
* Chiseled Pitstone
* Pitstone Pillar
* Pot of Chance, occasionally

Loot
------

Underground Ruins Pots use common\_pot\_loot as their loot table.
