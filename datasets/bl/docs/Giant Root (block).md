Giant Root ingame

**Giant Root** is the wood block of both natural and Root Pod-grown Giant Roots. It can be ground into Ground Roots, and is considered an alternative Plant Item.

Plant Support
---------------

Giant Root supports the placement and growth of basic plants.

Fuel
------

Giant Root can be used as fuel for the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace, smelting 1.5 items per root block.

Events
--------

Placed Giant Root will take on an alternative texture during the Spook seasonal event.
