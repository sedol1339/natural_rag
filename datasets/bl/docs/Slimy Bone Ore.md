Obtaining
-----------

### Generation
Slimy Bone Ore generates relatively commonly underground between Y-layers 128 and 45 within Betweenstone, largely in the Cavern layer.

### Drops
Slimy Bone Ore can drop uncommonly from Boulder Sprites.

Drops
-------

Slimy Bone Ore drops a Slimy Bone and 1-4 XP when broken. The block itself must be broken with a Silk Touch tool to be collected.
