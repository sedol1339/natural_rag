# Silt Beach

[Aside block] Silt Beach
------------------------

### Type

Natural

### Generation

Patchy Islands  
Swamplands

A very small Silt Beach in a Swamplands biome

**Silt Beaches** are flat terrain structures that generate along the edges of islands in Patchy Islands and sometimes Swamplands biomes, bordering bodies of Swamp Water. They are often wide and expansive in Patchy Islands biomes, sometimes connecting islands together, but are much smaller and more limited in Swamplands biomes.

Composition

Mobs
------

The following mobs can spawn naturally on Silt Beaches:

* Silt Crab, in Patchy Islands biomes only
* Bubbler Crab, in Swamplands biomes only
