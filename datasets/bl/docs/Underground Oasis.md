**Underground Oases** are terrain structures that generate in the Cavern and Lake Cavern layers. They are small caves featuring a luscious patch of grass with various plants, a ceiling covered in stalactites, and a central monster spawner.

Despite their risks, Underground Oases can be aÂ relief to players lost underground in search of restoring hunger or decay, and may also be prime places to set up mob farms.

Composition
-------------

Underground Oases consist of:

* Betweenstone, as the walls of the cave
* Swamp Grass, on the floor
* Swamp Dirt, between the grass and cave walls
* Stalactite, hanging from the ceiling
* Swamp Tallgrass, commonly on the grass
* Double Swamp Tallgrass, occasionally on the grass
* Bulb-Capped Mushroom, occasionally on the grass
* Weeping Blue, occasionally on the grass
* Monster Spawner, in the center of the cave

Mobs
------

The Monster Spawner in an Underground Oasis is capable of spawning Swamp Hags, Wights, Blood Snails, Termites, or Leeches.

Structures
------------

Giant Bulb-Capped Mushrooms can rarely spawn within Underground Oases.
