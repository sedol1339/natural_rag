Weedwood Ladders ingame

**Weedwood Ladders** are craftable utility blocks that are the equivalent of vanilla Ladders for the Betweenlands.

Climbing
----------

Weedwood Ladders can be placed anywhere on the sides of blocks. They can then be climbed up and down like vanilla Ladders. However, they cannot be roped downwards like Climbing Rope.
