Blocks of Octine ingame

**Blocks of Octine** are craftable storage blocks for Octine Ingots. In addition to being crafted, they can also be found as part of Idol Head Statues and a secret room in the Wight Fortress.

Igniting
----------

Placed Moss, Cave Moss, Lichen, and Thorns that are adjacent to or on a Block of Octine will catch fire instantly.

Updrafts
----------

If a player using a Volarkite glides closely above a Block of Octine, the heat will create an updraft that pushes them upwards, giving them a constant vertical boost for about a second.

Healing
---------

A tamed Emberling on top of a Block of Octine will be healed by 1 health () every minute.
