**Ground Dried Swamp Reed** is a ground item that acts as the The Betweenlands equivalent of vanilla Bone Meal, capable of accelerating the growth of certain plants and crops in the dimension.

Plant Growth
--------------

Ground Dried Swamp Reed can applied to Swamp Grass by right-clicking, randomly sprouting basic plants nearby, including Swamp Tallgrass, Double Swamp Tallgrass, Cattails, Tall Cattails, and Shoots.

Ground Dried Swamp Reed can also be applied to Weedwood, Rubber Tree, Sap Tree, Hearthgrove, Nibbletwig, and Spirit Tree Saplings, as well as Root Pods, to help expedite their growth, along with White Pear Bushes and Yellow Dotted Fungus. However, it *cannot* be applied to Aspectrus Plants.

Infusing
----------

Ground Dried Swamp Reed has one or more Aspects tied to it, which means it can be used to make Infusions within the Infuser. Visit the Ground Items page for more information regarding the infusing feature.

Compostability
----------------

Ground Dried Swamp Reed has a compost process time of 3 minutes and 20 seconds and a compost amount of 3. Visit the Compost page for more information regarding the composting mechanic.
