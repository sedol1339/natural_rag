Polished Dentrothyst Panes ingame

**Polished Dentrothyst Panes** are craftable, thin translucent blocks. There are two varieties - Green and Orange.

Collection
------------

When harvested normally, Polished Dentrothyst Panes drop nothing. The blocks themselves must be harvested with a Silk Touch tool to be collected.
