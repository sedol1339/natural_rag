### Water Filtering
Sulfur can be obtained from filtering Stagnant Water with a Mesh Strainer in the Water Filter.

### Drops
Sulfur drops from breaking Sulfur Ore with any pickaxe.

It also drops from Pyrads upon death.

### Loot
Sulfur can be found as Loot from:

* Pots of Chance in the Cavern and Lake Cavern layers, Marsh Ruins, Underwater Ruins, Underground Ruins, Cragrock Towers, and the Wight Fortress.
* Urns of Chance in Sludge Plains Ruins and the Sludgeon Tower, Labyrinthine Vaults, Barrishee Lair, and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcoves in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Weedwood Chests in Idol Head Statues, Shrines, Cragrock Towers, the Wight Fortress, and the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.

Uses
------

### Fuel
Sulfur can be used as fuel for any Furnace (including the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace), smelting 8 items per piece.

It can also be used as fuel for several other utility blocks:

* For the Purifier, purifying 1 item per piece.
* For the Animator, animating 1 item for a varying number of pieces (depending on the recipe).
* For the Censer, providing about 5 minutes and 18 seconds of effect time per piece.
