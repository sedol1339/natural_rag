**Bottled Fog** is an item that can be placed in the Censer to speed up plant growth and grow Sludgeon plants outside of the Sludgeon in a small radius.

Description
-------------

Bottled Fog can be collected by right clicking an empty Dentrothyst Vial on the fog found on the sixth and seventh floors of the Labryinthine Vaults below the Sludgeon.

It can also be stored in Barrels.
