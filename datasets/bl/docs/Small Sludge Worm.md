**Small Sludge Worms** are hostile worm mobs that are spawned by Worm Holes in the first and second layers of the Labyrinthine Vaults of the Sludgeon. They can also spawn from Tunnel Entrances on the surface anywhere in The Betweenlands during the Heavy Rain event.

Description
-------------

Small Sludge Worms are brown in color. They can be spawned in groups of 2-4 from Worm Holes when a player comes near.
