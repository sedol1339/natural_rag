Stalactites ingame

**Stalactites** are unique twisting stone blocks that generate underground in Caverns. They also drop from stalactite-headed Boulder Sprites and can be found as part of Underground Oases.

Contents

* 1 Placement

Placement
-----------

Placed Stalactites connect vertically with each other, forming elongated stalactite or stalagmite formations that grow smaller the further they are to the tip.

Collection
------------

Stalactites drop nothing when harvested. The blocks themselves cannot be collected from harvesting in any way.
