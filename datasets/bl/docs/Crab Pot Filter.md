Usage
-------

Must be placed underwater. With a Crab Pot containing a Silt or Bubbler Crab placed on top, a Crab Pot Filter can be fueled with Anadia Remains to Filter various items.

### In-game Tooltip
Used with a Crab Pot. Crab Pots placed above, containing Silt or Bubbler Crabs, provide extra functions.
