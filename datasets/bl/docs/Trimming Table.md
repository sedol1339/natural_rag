Properties
------------

### Trimming
A Bone Axe is required to operate the Trimming Table.

### Trimming Outputs
Trimming a Bubbler Crab or Silt Crab results in 2 Crab Claws and 2 Crabsticks.

Trimming an Anadia has a variety of potential outputs. After chopping a fish, 3 outputs are shown. After taking the desired outputs, the player can choose to void the rest in exchange for Anadia Remains.
