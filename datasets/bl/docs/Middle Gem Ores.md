Middle Gem Ores ingame

**Middle Gem Ores** are special ore blocks that can be purified into Middle Gems in a Purifier. The resulting Middle Gems can then be combined with tools and armor or crafted into Amulets for a buff depending on the chosen gem (see the Middle Gem Circle page for more details).

There are three varieties: **Aqua**, **Crimson**, and **Green**.

Obtaining
-----------

### Generation
Middle Gem Ores generate rarely as single blocks underwater within Mud in any biome on the surface of The Betweenlands, though most commonly in Sludge Plains biomes.

Properties
------------

### Detection
Middle Gem Ores can be detected through blocks by using a Gem Singer.

### Events
Placed Middle Gem Ores will take on alternative textures during the Winter seasonal event.
