Swamplands

A group of Nibbletwig Trees

**Nibbletwig Trees** are a type of tree that generates commonly in Swamplands biomes. They can be anywhere between 7 and 15 blocks tall and are around 5 blocks wide. They feature a thin, spindly trunk supporting small clusters of ivy-drooping leaves, and a small, pointed top canopy.

Nibbletwig Trees are particularly useful for their logs, which can be crafted into edible, experience-granting sticks.

Composition
-------------

Nibbletwig Trees consist of:

* Nibbletwig Log, as the log block
* Nibbletwig Leaves, as the leaf block
* Poison Ivy, hanging from the leaves

Growing
---------

Nibbletwig Trees can be grown with Nibbletwig Saplings.
