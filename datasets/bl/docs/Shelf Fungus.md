Obtaining
-----------

### Generation
Shelf Fungus generates in ring-like shapes around Giant Weedwood Trees and natural Giant Roots.

Properties
------------

### Spawning
Shelf Fungus supports the spawning of Sporelings in all biomes in The Betweenlands, *except* for Sludge Plains biomes and the Swamplands Clearing and Sludge Plains Clearing sub-biomes.

### Events
Placed Shelf Fungus will take on alternative textures during the Spook and Winter seasonal events.

Uses
------

### Fuel
Shelf Fungus can be used as fuel for the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace, smelting 1.5 items per fungus block.
