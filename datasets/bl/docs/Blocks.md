**Minerals**
------------

* Betweenlands Bedrock: The Bedrock of the Betweenlands, found at the world's bottom.
* Betweenstone: The primary stone of the Betweenlands, used in many recipes.
* Coarse Swamp Dirt: A version of Swamp Dirt that grass cannot spread to.
* Compacted Mud: A version of Mud that does not cause sinking.
* Cragrock: A bluish stone that is found in Coarse Islands and as part of spires in Deep Waters.
* Dentrothysts: An amber-like crystal found within Giant Weedwood Trees.
* Life Crystal Stalactite: Pitstone spikes containing white ore found in the Lake Caverns.
* Limestone: A white stone that spawns in large patches in the Betweenlands Caverns.
* Middle Gem Ores: A variety of colored gems found within Mud in the Betweenlands.
* Mud: A thick, entity-sinking block found everywhere in the Betweenlands.
* Octine Ore: An uncommon, flaming orange ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
* Pitstone: A dark stone that spawns deep in the Lake Caverns of the Betweenlands.
* Scabyst Ore: A somewhat rare pale blue ore found deep underground in the Betweenlands.
* Silt: A slowing, sand-like block found on beaches in Patchy Islands.
* Slimy Bone Ore: A relatively common fossil ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
* Sludgy Dirt: A slowing brown block found in patches in Sludge Plains.
* Solid Tar: A dense, solidified block of tar created when Swamp Water and Tar meet.
* Stalactite: Twisty stone spikes that spawn in the Caverns of the Betweenlands.
* Sulfur Ore: A common yellow ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
* Syrmorite Ore: An uncommon blue ore found underground in the Betweenlands.
* Swamp Dirt: The primary dirt block of the Betweenlands.
* Valonite Ore: A rare pinkish ore found deep underground in the Betweenlands.

**Liquids**

* Puddle: Thin blocks of Swamp Water left behind by Heavy Rain.

**Utility**
-----------

* Alembic: A block capable of distilling Infusion Buckets into completed Infusions and Aspect Vials.
* Animator: A block capable of using the power of Life Crystals to 'animate' certain items.
* Aspect Vials: Vials containing a specific aspect usable in infusing.
* Barrels: Blocks capable of storing and carrying any liquid.
* Buckets: Can be placed to tap the logs of Rubber Trees for liquid rubber.
* Buttons: Functioning buttons for the Betweenlands.
* Censer: A block capable of producing ground-laying fog from various sources, with varying effects.
* Climbing Rope: A block that can be climbed on and 'roped down'.
* Compost Bin: A block capable of turning plant matter into Compost to use in farming.
* Crab Pot: A block capable of catching Silt and Bubbler Crabs with a lure.
* Crab Pot Filter:A block allowing caged Crabs to filter certain items.
* Dark Druid Altar: The altar in the middle of the Druid Circle that assembles the Swamp Talisman.
* Dentrothyst Vials: Containers for Infusions.
* Doors: Functioning doors for the Betweenlands.
* Dual Sulfur Furnace: A variant of the Sulfur Furnace that can smelt twice as many items at once.
* Dug Purified Swamp Dirt: Created by digging a hole in Purified Swamp Dirt with a shovel.
* Dug Purified Swamp Grass: Created when grass spreads to Dug Purified Swamp Dirt.
* Dug Swamp Dirt: Created by digging a hole in Swamp Dirt with a shovel.
* Dug Swamp Grass: Created by digging a hole in Swamp Grass with a shovel.
* Fence Gates: Functioning fence gates for the Betweenlands.
* Fish Trimming Table: A block capable of trimming certain caught mobs.
* Fishing Tackle Box: A small portative chest the player can place and sit on.
* Gecko Cage: A block capable of housing a gecko which can be tested on to discover Aspects.
* Glowing Goop: A light source that can be thrown.
* Infuser: A block capable of mixing various ingredients together to make raw Infusion Buckets.
* Item Shelf: A storage block that can display and store multiple items.
* Lanterns: Light sources for the Betweenlands that, unlike torches, are not extinguished by rain.
* Menhir: A naturally generating monolith that can be linked to act as a waypoint.
* Mortar: A block capable of grinding many materials into their ground forms.
* Moss Bed: A bed that sets a player's spawn point in the Betweenlands.
* Mud Brick Flower Pot: A Betweenlands variant of the Flower Pot that can also be used as a candle.
* Pressure Plates: Functioning pressure plates for the Betweenlands.
* Purifier: A block capable of 'purifying' certain blocks into new forms.
* Repeller: A block that can keep mobs away by putting up a repelling shield.
* Silt Glass Jar: A block used to store Fish Bait.
* Simulacra: Naturally generating statuettes that contain mysterious effects.
* Smoking Rack: A block capable of smoking various types of food.
* Smoldering Peat: Created by lighting Peat on fire.
* Sulfur Furnace: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Furnace.
* Sulfur Torch: Torches that function properly in the Betweenlands, but are extinguished by rain.
* Syrmorite Hopper: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Hopper.
* Trapdoors: Functioning trapdoors for the Betweenlands.
* Weedwood Chest: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Chest.
* Weedwood Crafting Table: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Crafting Table.
* Weedwood Jukebox: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Jukebox.
* Weedwood Ladder: A functioning ladder for the Betweenlands.
* Weedwood Lever: A functioning lever for the Betweenlands.
* Weedwood Sign: A writable Sign for the Betweenlands.
* Wind Chime: A block capable of predicting most Events in the Betweenlands.

**Generated**
-------------

* Angry Betweenstone: Betweenstone etched with the symbol of the mod's creators.
* Bauble: Experience-filled ornaments that randomly appear and disappear on trees during Winter.
* Beam Lens: Transmits beams from the origin and can be reoriented to direct the beam a certain way.
* Beam Origin: A block that generates a beam for the puzzle on top of the Sludgeon Tower.
* Beam Passage: Allows beams from the origin to pass through from above, but cannot be reoriented.
* Black Ice: Frozen, slippery swamp water that spreads during Winter.
* Brazier: Can be lit on fire to activate the Beam Origin at the top of the Sludgeon Tower.
* Broken Mud Tiles: A block that crumbles into Stagnant Water when walked on, found in the Sludgeon.
* Carved Rotten Bark: Rotten Bark etched with decorative runes, found in the Sludgeon.
* Decayed Mud Tiles: Blocks that are slippery to walk on, found in the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcove: Alcoves in the walls of the Sludgeon that can contain urns with loot.
* Dungeon Door: Massive doors that can be opened using a rune combination.
* Dungeon Door Code: Contains the rune combination that must be used to open Dungeon Doors.
* Energy Barrier: Blocks that guard certain areas of the Wight Fortress and the Sludgeon.
* Glowing Betweenstone Tile: A decorative light source found in the Wight Fortress.
* Glowing Smooth Cragrock: A decorative light source found in Cragrock Towers.
* Item Cage: An energy-imbued cage used in a special ritual, found in the Wight Fortress.
* Mirages: Blocks that look ordinary but can be passed through, used to conceal specific secrets.
* Monster Spawner: The Betweenlands equivalent of the normal Monster Spawner.
* Offering Table: Stands of offering containing loot, which have a special connection with Simulacra.
* Possessed Block: A block housing a terrifying jumpscare, found in the Wight Fortress.
* Pot of Chance: Pots containing various loot, found in Caverns and structures in the Betweenlands.
* Present: Gift-filled presents that randomly appear and disappear during Winter.
* Puffshroom: A block housing a fungal surprise, found in the Sludgeon.
* Rotten Support Beam: Decorative support beams found in the Sludgeon.
* Rune Stone: Overworld Stone etched with various symbols, found in the Druid Circle.
* Runged Mud Bricks: Brown brick blocks that can be climbed on, found in the Sludgeon.
* Sludge: A slowing cover block left behind by Sludges as they hop about.
* Snow: A cover block that builds up during Winter snowfall.
* Spike Traps: Blocks that house deadly spikes, found in the Wight Fortress and the Sludgeon.
* Urn of Chance: Urns containing various loot, found in the Sludgeon and other mud-based structures.
* Wall Candle: Light sources that can be lit or unlit, found in the Sludgeon.
* Weak Blocks: Blocks that break when walked upon, found mainly in the Wight Fortress.
* Wisp: Colored flames found eerily in the Betweenlands.
