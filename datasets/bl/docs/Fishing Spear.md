Loot
------

Fishing and Amphibious Fishing Spears can be obtained as loot from Greebling Coracles.

Usage
-------

Holding right click allows the player to throw the fishing spear. Amphibious and Robust Fishing Spears will glide through water.

Animating
-----------

Robust Amphibious Fishing Spears can be animated. This gives them the ability to return to the player after being thrown.

| Result | Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Animated Robust Amphibious Fishing Spear | Robust Amphibious Fishing Spear + Sulfur (2) + Life Crystal (1% Charge) |  |

Repairing
-----------

Like most other equipment with durability, damaged Fishing Spears can be repaired to full durability through an Animator. The more that needs to be repaired, the greater the cost of fuel and crystal charge is in the animation process.

| Result | Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Fully repairs corresponding Fishing Spear | Any damaged Fishing Spear + Sulfur (3-6) + Life Crystal (4-13% charge) |  |
