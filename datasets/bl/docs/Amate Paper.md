### Drops
Amate Paper can drop uncommonly from Crypt Crawlers upon death.

It can also drop from looting Greebling Corpses.

### Loot
Amate Paper can be found as Loot from:

* Urns of Chance in Sludge Plains Ruins and the Sludgeon Tower, Labyrinthine Vaults, Barrishee Lair, and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcoves in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Weedwood Chests in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.
