# Release 3.6.0

The Betweenlands but we're vibing with some Greeblings in a rain storm

**Release 3.6.0** was a major update to the Betweenlands for Minecraft 1.12.2 that was released on May 7, 2020. It was known as the **Birdbats, Boats and Banter** update as it added the Raised Isles biome, the Draeton, the Chiromaw Matriarch mini-boss and tamable Chiromaws, as well as updating the Heavy Rain and Thunderstorm events and Weedwood Rowboats, alongside several other additions and changes.
