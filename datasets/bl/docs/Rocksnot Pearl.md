### Trimming
Rocksnot Pearls can be obtained from trimming any *treasure* Anadia item (that is not rotten) in the Trimming Table. The amount obtained per item depends on the Anadia's Size attribute, with larger sizes yielding more pearls.

### Drops
Rocksnot Pearls can drop rarely from Rock Snots upon death.

### Experience Collecting
One Rocksnot Pearl is produced by a *friendly* Rock Snot for every 20 XP that it collects from killing other mobs.

Properties
------------

### Consuming
When consumed, Rocksnot Pearls grant 10 XP to the player. They are consumed twice as fast as most other consumables.
