Usage
-------

Right-click with a Clean Silk Bundle in hand to open it. A Silk Bundle has 4 inventory slots that can each hold one item used in a Steeping recipe. Other items will not be accepted.

### Steeping
Put the item ingredients of a Steeping recipe in the bundle, then place it in a Steeping Pot with the fluid specified in the Steeping recipe.

After a recipe is completed or failed, a Dirty Silk Bundle is left behind, and must be purified before being used again.
