**Natural**
-----------

* Cavern: The Caverns of the Betweenlands, made up of Betweenstone and various cave flora.
* Chiromaw Matriarch Nest: A nest of giant winged beasts, often found on Raised Islands.
* Cragrock Spire: A mountainous formation of Cragrock found in Deep Waters.
* Giant Bulb-Capped Mushroom: A large glowing blue mushroom found in Patchy Islands.
* Giant Hollow Log: A large, rotten log of a fallen tree found in Swamplands.
* Giant Root: A large, overgrown and varying root formation found in Coarse Islands.
* Giant Weedwood Tree: A towering tree with a massive canopy and hanger vines.
* Hearthgrove Tree: A small, pale tree with an orange canopy found in Patchy Islands.
* Lake Cavern: The deepest part of the Caverns, made up of Pitstone and various cave flora.
* Nibbletwig Tree: A tall, brown tree with a dark green canopy found in Swamplands.
* Raised Island: A giant sky island supported by roots found throughout Raised Isles.
* Rotten Weedwood Tree: A deathly tree stripped of leaves found in Sludge Plains.
* Rubber Tree: An uncommon tree found in Swamplands that can be tapped.
* Sap Tree: An uncommon tree found in many Betweenlands locations that provides edible sap.
* Silt Beach: A coastal flat of silt found in Swamplands and Patchy Islands.
* Spirit Tree: A sentient, elderly tree whose power is determined by the wisps surrounding it.
* Underground Oasis: A patch of underground flora containing valuable natural resources.
* Weedwood Tree: The basic willow-like tree found commonly in the Betweenlands.

**Buildings**
-------------

* Cragrock Tower: An extremely tall, monolithic tower filled with valuable loot.
* Druid Circle: A structure in the Overworld that provides the needs to get to the Betweenlands.
* Idol Head Statue: A giant head of cragrock with buried loot, found in Patchy Islands.
* Ruins: Small ruins of betweenstone or mud bricks, found in Marshes and Sludge Plains.
* Shrine: A small shrine with a random spawner and loot, found in Marshes and Sludge Plains.
* Sludgeon: A massive, labyrinthine, sludgy dungeon housing the Pit of Decay.
* Tar Pool Dungeon: A structure with a Tar pool that spawns Tar Beasts, found underground.
* Underground Ruins: Small ruins of pitstone bricks that spawn in the Lake Caverns.
* Wight Fortress: A massive fortress dungeon housing the Primordial Malevolence.

**Post-Gen**
------------

* Portal Tree: The tree created with the Swamp Talisman that allows travel to the Betweenlands.
* Tunnel Entrances: Holes that appear in the ground during certain events and spawn mobs.

**Upcoming**
------------

* Ancient Temple: A large and expansive temple with guardians and bosses.
* Tribe Settlement: A village of tribals with quests.
