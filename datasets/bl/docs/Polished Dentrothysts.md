Polished Dentrothysts ingame

**Polished Dentrothysts** are smeltable translucent blocks. There are two varieties - Green and Orange.

Collection
------------

When harvested normally, Polished Dentrothysts drop nothing. The blocks themselves must be harvested with a Silk Touch tool to be collected.
