Obtaining
-----------

### Generation
Hollow Logs generate in fallen trunk formations in Swamplands, Marsh, and Sludge Plains biomes.

Drops
-------

When broken, Hollow Logs drop Dry Bark, which can then be ground into Ground Weedwood Bark. The blocks themselves cannot be collected in Survival Mode.

Uses
------

### Fuel
Hollow Logs can be used as fuel for any Furnace (including the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace), smelting 1.5 items per log.

### Compostability
Hollow Logs have a compost process time of 10 minutes and a compost amount of 25. Visit the Compost page for more information regarding the composting mechanic.
