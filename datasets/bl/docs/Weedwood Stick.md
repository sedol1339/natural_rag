### Drops
Weedwood Sticks drop from breaking Weedwood Bushes and the stick variant of Nesting Blocks. They also drop from breaking Dead Weedwood Bushes with a Sickle.

Additionally, Weedwood Sticks can drop from looting Greebling Corpses.

Uses
------

### Fuel
Weedwood Sticks can be used as fuel for any Furnace (including the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace), smelting 0.5 items per stick.
