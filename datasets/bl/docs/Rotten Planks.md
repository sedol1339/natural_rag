Rotten Planks ingame

**Rotten Planks** are craftable building blocks. In addition to being crafted, they can also be found as part of the Sludgeon Tower of the Sludgeon.

------

Rotten Planks can be used as fuel for the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace, smelting 1.5 items per plank block.
