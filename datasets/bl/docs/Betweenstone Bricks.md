Betweenstone Bricks ingame

**Betweenstone Bricks** are craftable building blocks. They have a normal variant, a Mossy variant, and a Cracked variant. In addition to being crafted, the normal variant can also be found as part of Marsh Ruins and Shrines, the Mossy variant can be found surrounding natural Spirit Trees, and all variants can be found as part of the Wight Fortress.
