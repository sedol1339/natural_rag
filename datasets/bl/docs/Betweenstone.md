Betweenstone ingame

**Betweenstone** is the primary stone block of The Betweenlands, generating abundantly underground in the upper Cavern layer. It also drops from Boulder Sprites, and can be found as part of Underground Oases, Tar Pool Dungeons, and the Wight Fortress.
