Weedwood ingame

**Weedwood** is the inner wood block of Weedwood Trees and Giant Weedwood Trees, as well as the base of the Portal Tree.

Fuel
------

Weedwood can be used as fuel for the Sulfur Furnace or Dual Sulfur Furnace, smelting 1.5 items per block.
