# Mechanics

There are a variety of unique mechanics in the Betweenlands mod.

* Aspects: The process of distilling natural aspects out of ingredients in Herblore.
* Corrosion: Causes Betweenlands tools and weapons to become less effective over time.
* Decay: Causes the player to rot away over time while in the Betweenlands.
* Food Sickness: Eating too much of the same food will give diminishing returns.
* Middle Gem Circle: A rock-paper-scissors mechanic revolving around Middle Gems.
* Radial Equipment Menu: The system for equipping special items onto the player.
* Stench: Certain sources cause the player to smell really, *really* bad.
