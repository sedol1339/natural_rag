# Rotten Weedwood Tree

*(Not to be confused with the normal Weedwood Tree, Giant Weedwood Tree, or Giant Hollow Log)*

### Generation

Sludge Plains

A couple of thick Rotten Weedwood Trees

**Rotten Weedwood Trees** are a rotten variant of Weedwood Tree that generates in Sludge Plains biomes. Unlike normal Weedwood Trees, they cannot be grown.

They are generally around 16 blocks tall and around 15 blocks wide, featuring a thick, sloped trunk with supporting roots and leafless branches.

Composition
-------------

Rotten Weedwood Trees consist of:

* Rotten Bark, as the outer bark, root and branch block
* Weedwood, as the inner trunk block
* Poison Ivy, occasionally hanging from the branches

Mobs
------

While no mobs in particular spawn in or around Rotten Weedwood Trees, breaking the Rotten Bark the tree is composed of has a chance to release a large Termite.
