Solid Tar ingame

**Solid Tar** is a block that is formed whenever liquid Tar comes into contact with water, including vanilla Water, Swamp Water, or Stagnant Water, whether either liquid is a source block or flowing. It can also be found as part of Tar Pool Dungeons.
