**Dentrothyst Shards** are items primarily used in making vials for aspects, infusions, and other fluids.

There are two varieties of Dentrothyst Shard: **Green** and **Orange**.

Obtaining
-----------

### Drops
Green and Orange Dentrothyst Shards drop from breaking Green and Orange Dentrothyst, respectively, with any pickaxe.

### Throwing Infusions
Green and Orange Dentrothyst Shards are left behind upon throwing any Infusion from a Green or Orange Dentrothyst Vial, respectively.

### Loot
Green and Orange Dentrothyst Shards can be found as Loot from:

* Pots of Chance in the Cavern and Lake Cavern layers, Marsh Ruins, Underwater Ruins, Underground Ruins, Tar Pool Dungeons, Cragrock Towers, and the Wight Fortress.
* Urns of Chance in Sludge Plains Ruins and the Sludgeon Tower, Labyrinthine Vaults, Barrishee Lair, and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Dungeon Alcoves in the Labyrinthine Vaults and Crypt of the Sludgeon.
* Weedwood Chests in Cragrock Towers, the Wight Fortress, and the Labyrinthine Vaults and Barrishee Lair of the Sludgeon.

They can also be found as scattered items in Chiromaw Matriarch Nests.
