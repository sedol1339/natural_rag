The **Prime Wight** is the currently unimplemented final boss of the Betweenlands mod, and is the ruler of the dimension. Defeating him will be the final step in freeing the Betweenlands of its corrupted state.

In life he was a powerful sorcerer who theorized that the power of the nether could be used as a tool for peace. He is responsible for the current state of the Betweenlands.
